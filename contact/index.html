Frequently Asked Questions About Consultations

Is the consultation really free?

Yes, 100% free. There are no hidden costs and no obligation to hire us. We believe everyone deserves to understand their legal options after an accident.

What should I bring to a consultation?

If possible, bring your police report, medical records and bills, photos of injuries or the accident scene, insurance information, and any questions you have. But don't delay the consultation if you don't have everything—we can help gather documents.

How long does a consultation take?

Usually 30-60 minutes, depending on the complexity of your case. We take whatever time is needed to thoroughly understand your situation and answer your questions.

Can I do a phone or video consultation?

Absolutely. We offer flexible options including in-person meetings at our office, phone consultations, and video calls. We'll work with whatever is most convenient for your situation.
