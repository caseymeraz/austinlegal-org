### H2: How Rideshare Accidents Differ from Regular Car Accidents

#### H3: Multiple Insurance Policies
- Driver's personal insurance
- Uber/Lyft contingent liability coverage
- Third-party driver's insurance

#### H3: Uber and Lyft's Coverage Periods
- **Period 0:** App off – personal insurance only
- **Period 1:** App on, waiting for ride request – limited coverage
- **Period 2:** Ride accepted, en route to passenger – full coverage
- **Period 3:** Passenger in vehicle – full coverage ($1 million)

#### H3: Determining Liability
- Complex fault analysis
- Multiple potential defendants
- Corporate vs. contractor issues

2. **Document everything** – Photos, witness info
3. **Get a police report**
4. **Screenshot your Uber/Lyft app** – Trip details
5. **Get driver's information** – Name, license, insurance
6. **Seek medical attention** – Even if you feel fine
7. **Don't give recorded statements** to insurance
