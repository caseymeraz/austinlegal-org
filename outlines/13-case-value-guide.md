### H2: Average Settlement Ranges by Injury Type

*Note: These are general ranges; every case is different*

#### H3: Minor Injuries (Soft Tissue)
- Whiplash, strains, sprains
- Range: $10,000 - $25,000
- Factors affecting value

#### H3: Moderate Injuries
- Fractures, herniated discs
- Range: $25,000 - $100,000
- Surgery impact on value

#### H3: Serious Injuries
- TBI, spinal injuries
- Range: $100,000 - $500,000+
- Long-term care considerations

#### H3: Catastrophic Injuries
- Paralysis, amputation
- Range: $500,000 - $10,000,000+
- Lifetime care calculations

- Highly variable
- Depends on deceased's income

### H2: What Decreases Case Value

#### H3: Pre-Existing Conditions
- Insurance will argue causation
- Can be overcome with evidence

#### H3: Gaps in Treatment
- Suggests injury wasn't serious
- Follow all medical advice

#### H3: Social Media Activity
- Posts contradicting injuries
- Delete nothing, post nothing

#### H3: Comparative Negligence
- Your percentage of fault reduces recovery
- Texas 51% bar rule

#### H3: Low Insurance Limits
- Can't get blood from stone
- Explore all coverage options
