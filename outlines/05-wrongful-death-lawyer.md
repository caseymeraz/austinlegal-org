### H2: What is a Wrongful Death Claim in Texas?

#### H3: Definition of Wrongful Death
- Death caused by another's negligence, carelessness, or intentional act
- Civil claim separate from criminal proceedings

#### H3: Who Can File a Wrongful Death Claim?
- Surviving spouse
- Children (including adopted)
- Parents
- Filed individually or together

#### H3: Wrongful Death vs. Survival Action
- Wrongful death: family's losses
- Survival action: deceased's estate claims
- Both may be pursued

### H2: Texas Wrongful Death Laws

#### H3: Statute of Limitations
- 2 years from date of death
- Exceptions (discovery, minors)
- Link to: `/texas-statute-of-limitations/`

#### H3: Who Can Recover Damages?
- Spouse, children, parents only
- Siblings cannot file wrongful death claims

#### H3: Survival Actions
- Filed by estate representative
- Recovers deceased's damages

#### H3: Damage Caps
- No caps on most wrongful death damages
- Medical malpractice cap exceptions

#### H3: Comparative Negligence
- Deceased's partial fault impact
