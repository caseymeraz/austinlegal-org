- Size/weight differential (80,000 lbs vs. 4,000 lbs)
- Higher fatality and serious injury rates

#### H3: Multiple Liable Parties
- Truck driver
- Trucking company
- Cargo loading company
- Truck/parts manufacturer
- Maintenance provider

#### H3: Federal Regulations Apply
- FMCSA regulations
- Hours of service rules
- Maintenance requirements
- Driver qualification standards

#### H3: Larger Insurance Policies

#### H3: Rapid Evidence Destruction
