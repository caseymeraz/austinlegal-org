### H2: What to Do After a Motorcycle Accident

2. **Move to safety** if possible
3. **Document everything** – Photos/videos
4. **Get witness information**
5. **Seek medical attention** – Even if you feel okay
6. **Don't admit fault** – Let investigation determine fault
7. **Contact a motorcycle accident lawyer**
