### H2: Texas Medical Malpractice Laws

#### H3: Statute of Limitations
- 2 years from act/omission
- Discovery rule (2 years from discovery)
- 10-year statute of repose
- Link to: `/texas-statute-of-limitations/`

#### H3: Expert Report Requirement
- Must file expert report within 120 days
- Report must detail standard of care violation
- Failure = case dismissal

#### H3: Damage Caps in Texas
- Non-economic damages capped
- $250,000 per defendant (physicians)
- $250,000-$500,000 for hospitals
- No cap on economic damages

#### H3: Pre-Suit Notice Requirements
- 60-day notice to healthcare providers
- Tolling of limitations during notice period
