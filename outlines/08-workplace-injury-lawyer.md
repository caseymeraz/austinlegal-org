### H2: Steps After a Workplace Injury

1. **Report the injury immediately** to supervisor
2. **Seek medical attention** (authorized provider for workers' comp)
3. **Document everything** - photos, witness names
4. **File workers' comp claim** within 30 days
5. **Don't sign anything** without legal review
6. **Contact a workplace injury lawyer**
