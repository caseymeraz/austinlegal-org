Austin, Texas

Our home base is downtown Austin, where we serve injury victims from every corner of the city.

Downtown Austin

The heart of Austin sees heavy traffic around Congress Avenue, I-35, and the central business district. We handle accidents involving:

  • I-35 downtown corridor collisions
  • Congress Avenue traffic accidents
  • Construction zone injuries (ongoing development)
  • Pedestrian accidents near entertainment districts
  • Rideshare accidents (6th Street area)

North Austin

Rapid development in North Austin has created challenging traffic conditions:

  • Highway 183 accidents
  • The Domain area incidents
  • Tech corridor workplace injuries
  • MoPac (Loop 1) collisions

South Austin

Growing suburban areas and major highways create significant accident risks:

  • I-35 South corridor accidents
  • Manchaca Road area collisions
  • South Congress (SoCo) pedestrian accidents
  • Ben White Boulevard incidents

East Austin

West Austin

Round Rock

As Williamson County's largest city, Round Rock has seen explosive growth—and with it, increased accident risks on its roads.

Round Rock Personal Injury Cases

Common accident locations and types we handle in Round Rock include:

  • I-35 Corridor: One of the busiest stretches in Texas, with frequent accidents near major exits
  • Highway 79: Heavy traffic leading to Taylor and Hutto
  • Dell Diamond Area: Event-related traffic and parking lot accidents
  • Premium Outlets: Pedestrian and parking lot incidents

Williamson County Courts

We regularly practice in Williamson County courts and are familiar with local procedures, judges, and opposing counsel. This local knowledge helps us navigate your case efficiently and effectively.

Cedar Park

Cedar Park's rapid growth has created new roads, new hazards, and new legal challenges for accident victims.

Cedar Park Personal Injury Cases

  • Highway 183A Toll Road: High-speed accidents on this major north-south artery
  • FM 1431 (Whitestone Boulevard): Heavy retail traffic and intersection collisions
  • H-E-B Center at Cedar Park: Event traffic and parking lot accidents
  • Retail and restaurant districts: Slip and falls, premises liability

Growing Community, Growing Risks

Cedar Park's ongoing development means construction zones, changing traffic patterns, and infrastructure adjustments. These transitions often create dangerous conditions for motorists, cyclists, and pedestrians.

Pflugerville

One of the fastest-growing cities in Texas, Pflugerville faces unique challenges as infrastructure struggles to keep pace with population growth.

Pflugerville Personal Injury Cases

  • Highway 130 Toll Road: High-speed accidents on this major corridor
  • FM 685: Heavy commuter traffic with frequent accidents
  • SH 45: Toll road incidents connecting to I-35
  • Heatherwilde Boulevard: Retail and residential area accidents

Rapid Growth Challenges

New roads, new intersections, and constant construction create learning curves for drivers—and opportunities for accidents. We help Pflugerville residents recover full compensation when negligence causes injuries.

Georgetown

The Williamson County seat combines historic charm with modern growth, creating diverse personal injury situations.

Georgetown Personal Injury Cases

  • I-35 Accidents: Major interstate collisions
  • Downtown Square: Pedestrian accidents and premises liability
  • Sun City: Accidents involving older adult drivers and pedestrians
  • Wolf Ranch: Retail area incidents

Serving Williamson County

We handle cases in Georgetown's courts and throughout Williamson County. Our familiarity with local procedures and personnel helps us advocate effectively for our clients.

We Come to You

Can't Travel to Our Office?

We understand that after a serious accident, traveling to our downtown Austin office may be difficult or impossible. That's why we offer flexible consultation options throughout our service area:

  • Hospital visits: If you're hospitalized in Austin, Round Rock, or elsewhere in Central Texas, we'll come to you
  • Home consultations: We'll meet you at your home if mobility is an issue
  • Flexible meeting locations: We can arrange meetings at locations convenient to you
  • Phone and video consultations: For those who prefer remote meetings

Your recovery comes first. We'll work around your needs and schedule to ensure you get the legal help you deserve.
